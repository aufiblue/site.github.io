About author

How did I discover Pen and Paper?

About nine months ago, I listened to a podcast called “Zeitsprung” which recorded a podcast episode together with someone else called “3W6”. I was interested in it and this is how I came across this podcast. It deals with the topic of Pen and Paper and I listened to some episodes.

What happened afterwards?

So I quickly came across other Pen and Paper podcasts like “Rookie’s Die”, “Dungeon Starter” or “Pen and Paper” from the Rocketbeans. Since I was now very motivated to start by myself, I looked for role-playing games on the internet. After a relatively short time “Aborea” fell into my hands. I was immediately enthusiastic.

Why an own website?

After I received “Aborea” and played with it for a short time with my game round, I started to invent a few expansions myself. Little by little there were more and more. Couldn’t I reveal these expansions to the public? And so it happened!

    Aborea - Attention Werwolf!

    Have a nice day to you all and have fun with “Aborea - Attention Werewolf! “Aborea - Attention Werewolf!” is an extension for the tabletop roleplaying game Aborea. It is about the fact that on the last day of every month some rational beings with a weak immune system become werewolves and try to destroy all life. However, at the beginning of the next month they become normal again and cannot remember anything!

    Dice cards

    Dice cards - What is that? Whoever does not have a certain dice at hand, but needs it to be able to play, can download these alternative “dice”. For example, if you need a W6, you can cut out the dice cards with the required numbers and throw them into a container. If you want to throw the dice, reach in without looking and pull out one of the dice cards.
