Aborea - Attention Werwolf!

Have a nice day to you all and have fun with “Aborea - Attention Werewolf! “Aborea - Attention Werewolf!” is an extension for the roleplaying game Aborea. It is about the fact that on the last day of every month some rational beings with a weak immune system become werewolves and try to destroy all life. However, at the beginning of the next month they become normal again and cannot remember anything!

Dice cards

Dice cards - What is that? Whoever does not have a certain dice at hand, but needs it to be able to play, can download these alternative “dice”. For example, if you need a W6, you can cut out the dice cards with the required numbers and throw them into a container. If you want to throw the dice, reach in without looking and pull out one of the dice cards.
